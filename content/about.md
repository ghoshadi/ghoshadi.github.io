I am a Ph.D. student in Statistics at Stanford University, advised by
[Stefan Wager](https://web.stanford.edu/~swager/) and
[Dominik Rothenhäusler](https://sites.google.com/view/rothenhaeusler/home).

My research spans causal inference, statistical learning, optimization, policy evaluation in dynamical systems, and distribution shift.

Besides research, I like teaching and mentoring younger students. I previously maintained a blog aimed at helping high school students prepare for Mathematical Olympiads and similar competitions.

In my free time, I enjoy travel, photography, cooking, chess, and movies.
